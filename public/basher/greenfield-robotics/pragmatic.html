Hardware company with significant per-robot manufacturing cost, but no CapEx schedule or unit build cost disclosed. This is a critical gap for a fleet-scaling model.
P5Survival runway without new fundingFail
At ~$150K/month burn against $16K/month in revenue, the company has essentially no self-sustaining runway. Survival is entirely dependent on continuous external capital raises. Far below the 36-month benchmark.
P6First critical hireIncomplete
Not addressed in available materials. Given the financial gap between burn and revenue, a CFO or VP Operations with hardware-as-a-service scaling experience is likely the blocking hire — but no pipeline or role is identified.
P7Unit economics todayFail
Gross margin of ~7% on $196K revenue is deeply problematic. The cost of deploying and operating each robot consumes nearly all the service fee. No CAC or per-robot LTV is disclosed. Unit economics are not viable at current scale.
P8Single point of failureIncomplete
Not explicitly named, though several obvious candidates exist: dependence on Chipotle/MKC as anchor investors, robot manufacturing supply chain, and the founder's personal role as operator. None are addressed with mitigation plans.
P9Regulatory or legal gatePass
Autonomous mechanical robots operating in open agricultural fields carry minimal regulatory burden compared to chemical alternatives. No FDA, EPA, or OSHA gates have been identified as blocking factors — a genuine advantage over herbicide-based competitors.
P10Revenue concentration riskIncomplete
With 20 robots across "a few dozen farmers" in Kansas, revenue concentration among a handful of customers is likely high, but not quantified. If top 2-3 farm contracts were lost, impact on the already-thin revenue base could be severe.
P11Founder proof of executionPass
Clint Brauer left a high-paying Los Angeles tech career, returned to Kansas, taught himself agriculture, built working robots from scratch over seven years, deployed a commercial fleet, and attracted Chipotle and a major ag cooperative as investors — all without traditional VC backing. That is a genuine and specific track record of execution.
